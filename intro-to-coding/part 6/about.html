Archive of Nostalgic Objects

A crowdsourced collection of media and objects we hold dear

List View About

The Nostalgic Objects project started as an informal survey sent out July 2024. Participants were asked "What is your favorite 'nostalgic' item?" Objects could be in any medium or format, including but not limited to physical objects, web experiences, songs, shows, games, old school assignments, etc. The only limitations were that participants must have actually interacted or used these objects before.

Nostalgic Objects is an experimental research project by Adhiti C and Eileen L. They are interested in the relationship people have with media, and its impact on their feelings and memories.
